About Us

Underwatch is a non-profit eSports organization, which strives to create a safe space for all players! Our goal is to connect you with other like-minded people and thereby improve your gameplay. With the help of our supporters, mods and team managers, we are able to bring to you 100% free teams with professional coaches. This would not be possible without our donors, so thank you to them again! For people who don’t want to, or aren’t ready, to join a competitive team, we offer a vivid community of Overwatch players, with multiple events and tournaments every single week.
